Our Background

We have been established for 34 years and we aim to give our customers the best value possible - quality parts at competitive prices. We keep a constant check on quality and fit. Check the F.A.Q. page for regular tips and advice. Our company firmly believes that "The customer comes first" and we are committed to constant updating of our product range and customer service. We are based in Redditch, Worcestershire, U.K. Our online catalogue and some historic information incorrectly gives our address as being in Birchfield Road, Redditch. Should you wish to send any item by carrier or postal services, please contact us for our current business address. Please note that we operate solely as a mail order business and for practical reasons we are unable to meet customers at our premises.
